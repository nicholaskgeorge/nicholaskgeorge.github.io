Purpose and Interests

Motivations

One of my primary reasons for studying ECE was the freedom I felt it gave me for personal pursuits. No other major felt as though it both gave me the tools to build any idea which comes to me. The ability to come home one day and out of the blue constuct say, a very crude computer out of simple components facinated me. Much of personal project come from random cool ideas I thought of one the spot. My general interest focus on embedded system design and analog/digital ciruit design and the intersection between them.
